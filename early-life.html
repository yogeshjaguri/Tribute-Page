Early Life of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, in a humble family. He faced numerous challenges during his early years, but his determination and passion for learning pushed him to overcome obstacles and pursue his dreams.

As a young boy, Kalam showed a keen interest in science and technology. He excelled in his studies and went on to graduate from the Madras Institute of Technology with a degree in Aeronautical Engineering.

The foundation of Dr. Kalam's illustrious career was laid during his formative years, where he developed a strong work ethic, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
